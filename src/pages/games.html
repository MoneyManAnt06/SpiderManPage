Did you know this about comics...?

to the Velislav Bible

This 14th century manuscript is considered the first comic in history and includes 747 illustrations showing the Old Testament. This work was carried out so that the population with a large number of illiterates could understand the Bible through drawings.

Hulk's color

At first, Hulk was going to be gray but due to printing color problems it was changed to green. The precision of the printing presses was not so great at that time

Mario Puzo, I try to write comics

The Godfather writer tried several times to write comics for Marvel, but was rejected due to lack of creativity. Who knows if he had continued what types of comics he would have released

Weak points

Another curiosity that many have not stopped at is that most superheroes, all in the case of Marvel, have a weak point that exposes them and leaves them vulnerable to enemies.

Comic and Hitler

Some of the most famous comics in history were part of North American propaganda against the advance of Nazism, and good proof of this is that, in his first appearance, Captain America is hitting none other than Adolf in the face. Hitler.

Superhero skills

His father and creator of other classics such as Spiderman, Ironman, Hulk or the X-Men, the great Stan Lee, hosted a reality show in the mid-2000s, whose objective was none other than to find people with talents or abilities typical of The Superheroes.
