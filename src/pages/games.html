Did you know this about comics...?

to the Velislav Bible

This 14th century manuscript is considered the first comic in history and includes 747 illustrations showing the Old Testament. This work was carried out so that the population with a large number of illiterates could understand the Bible through drawings.

Hulk's color

At first, Hulk was going to be gray but due to printing color problems it was changed to green. The precision of the printing presses was not so great at that time
